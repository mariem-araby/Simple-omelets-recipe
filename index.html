Simple omelette recipe

An easy quick dish, prefect for any meal. this classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

preparation time

  • Total Approximately 10 minutes
  • preparation 5 minutes
  • cooking 5minutes

Ingredient

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • pepper, to test
  • 1tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs : a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    will mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture
  2. Heat the pan: a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter ar oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): when the eggs begin to set at edges but are still slightly
    runny in middle, sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then it onto a plate
  6. Enjoy: hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
